About Me

Welcome.
I am a rising second-year graduate student pursuing a Master of Science in Computer Science at Georgia Tech and specializing in Artificial Intelligence (AI) & Machine Learning (ML).

As a young individual, I always had a passion for video games. I always wondered about the individuals that were able to create games with such intelligence. I recall playing games against computer-controlled bots, and wondering: 'How is this bot so smart?'. As I grew up, I knew that I wanted to pursue a career within computing because I wanted to learn more about the technologies that I grew up with.

Throughout my student career, I started learning more about how games developed smart game bots through the use of AI. I decided to pursue AI and ML with an undergraduate research project, which then led me to apply to graduate school within the field.

I am enjoying my journey so far, and I have learned about the many real-world problems that AI and ML has helped solve today. I look forward to joining an innovative company looking to solve problems through the use of AI and ML. Challenges can always arise with projects, but with a team one can overcome many barriers. I hope to join a team where software engineers can work together to solve problems that will impact many lives.

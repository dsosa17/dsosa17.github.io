About Me

Welcome.
I am a Data Science Software Engineer in the San Francisco Bay Area.

As a young individual, I was passionate about video games.
I always wondered about the geniuses that were able to create game bots with such intelligence.
I recall playing games against computer bots, and wondering: 'How is this bot so smart?'.
As I grew up, I knew that I wanted to pursue a career within computing because I wanted to learn about the technologies that I grew up with.

Throughout my student career, I learned about how games were developed with smart game bots through the use of AI.
With further interest in the subject, I decided to pursue AI and ML through an undergraduate research project, which then led me to apply to graduate school within the field.

It is interesting hearing about the real-world problems that AI and ML have helped solve today.
I look forward to joining an innovative company looking to solve problems through the use of software solutions.
Challenges can always arise with projects, but with a team one can overcome many barriers.
